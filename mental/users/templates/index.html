Mental Health matters a lot in our daily life

The total global mental health apps market was valued at $5.2 billion in 2022 and is expected to expand to $17 billion by 2030.

  • Mental health refers to the overall well-being of an individual's emotional, psychological, and social state. Good mental health is essential for a happy, healthy, and productive life.
  • Mental health problems can affect anyone, regardless of age, gender, race, or socio-economic status.
  • Mental health problems can include depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, eating disorders, substance abuse, and others.

It is important to prioritize mental health and seek help if needed. This can involve talking to a trusted friend or family member, seeking professional therapy or counseling, practicing self-care activities such as meditation or exercise, and taking steps to reduce stress and improve overall well-being. Remember, mental health matters and taking care of yourself is essential to living a fulfilling and happy life.

01

Depression

A mental health disorder characterized by persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and a loss of interest in daily activities.

02

Anxiety Disorders

A group of mental health disorders characterized by excessive worrying, nervousness, and fear that can interfere with daily activities.

03

Bipolar Disorder

A mental health disorder characterized by episodes of both depression and mania (elevated or irritable mood, increased energy, decreased need for sleep, and impulsive behavior).
